Full Policy

1. Information We Collect
Home Rhythm is built to respect your privacy. We do not require an account, nor do we collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifier. All data (rooms, tasks, custom photos, reminders, and app settings) is stored exclusively on your own device. Crash Reports and Analytics: We use Firebase Crashlytics (a Google service) to monitor the app's stability and improve performance. If the app crashes, Crashlytics automatically collects anonymous diagnostic data, such as your device model, operating system version, and the line of code that caused the error. This data is strictly used to fix bugs and is not linked to your personal identity.

2. In-App Purchases
We offer In‑App Purchases to unlock premium features. All payments are processed and secured by Apple’s App Store and Google Play Store. We never access, store, or transmit your payment information. Restore purchases functionality is provided as required by store guidelines.

3. Local Storage Only – No Ads, No Tracking
Home Rhythm does not use any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or third‑party tracking. No data ever leaves your device. The app does not share, sell, or transfer any information.

4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
Because we collect no personal information, there is no data to delete or disclose. If you are a California resident, you can rest assured that no sale or sharing of personal information takes place.
